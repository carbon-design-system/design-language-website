## Logo Requests

### IBM Logo use by IBMers

- The IBM logo is the intellectual property of IBM and it is one of our most
  valuable corporate assets. As IBMers, it is our responsibility to protect it.
  By requesting and downloading the IBM logo image files, you are acknowledging
  that you understand and will adhere to the IBM Logo guidelines, including:
- You are using the IBM logo for IBM sanctioned business purposes. Any other use
  of this logo image is strictly prohibited.
- You will not distribute the IBM logo file to third parties for their use. All
  third-party logo requests must be approved. Submit third-party requests
  through the
  [Logo Request Tool](https://prdpcrhibmbl01.w3-969.ibm.com/marketing/logotool/logotool.nsf/BrandingLogoHome?Openform)
  (w3 access is required).
- The IBM logo may not be used for any personal or commercial purposes. Email
  the following information to [branding@us.ibm.com]()
  - Your full name
  - Your IBM email address
  - A description of how you intend to use the IBM logo files

### IBM logo use by outside agency or third-party

We understand that our agency partners may require the IBM logo files in order
to create materials on behalf of IBM. Please ask your IBM sponsor or client for
the image files. They may also submit a request on your behalf using the
instructions above.

## Accessibility

IBM firmly believes that web and software experiences should be accessible to
everyone, regardless of abilities or impairments. Please visit our
[Equal Access Toolkit](https://www.ibm.com/able/toolkit/) that will help ensure
that your product or web experience meets or exceeds the standards for
accessibility.
